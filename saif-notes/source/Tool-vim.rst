vim
=====

1. Copy and Paste line

    .. code::

        To copy a line requires two commands: yy or Y ("yank") and either p ("put below") or P ("put above").

2. Cut line
      
    .. code::
        
        dd
        # you can paste it by..
        p

3. Cut from here to..

    .. code-block::

        # cut from here to next word
        dw 
        # cut from here to the end of the current word
        de
